ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Hans, a frontend developer based in Perth, Western Australia.

I have a passion for everything that lives on the internet, more specifically creating websites, applications or anything in between. One of my top goal is to always produce products that provide pixel-perfect, performant experiences and lastly, high quality.

My first bit of exposure to web development was at the North Metropolitan Tafe software development course. In there, we learned how to use HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript. I like to think that I’ve learned a lot since then!
